# Intro
I took AP World in my sophomore year of high school and going in, I had never taken an AP History class, so I wasn't sure what to expect. By the time May 7th came around, I was confident in my ability to get a 5 on that exam. I knew how to do the writing questions with ease and I could do the multiple choice section in 45 minutes. 

If you're gearing up to take AP World and you're in the same boat, don't worry. I'm a history geek, and I like to learn new things, so I found this class really interesting and fun to take. I'm going to list all the tips, resources, strategies, and methods that I did to really help me lock in throughout the school year to get a 5 on the AP World exam. I want to make it known that all the advice and my experiences are specific to myself and myself only. I cannot say you will get any specific score by solely doing what I did and you should feel free to do what you need with my advice.

Now that I've cleared any misconceptions, let's go!
